## ğŸ“‹ Overview

**What Did They Say?** is a gameplay-focused communication aid for World of Warcraft Classic. Unlike general-purpose translators, this addon is specifically designed to help players coordinate in groups, whisper, and communicate across language barriers during gameplay.

### Key Features

- âœ… **100% Offline** - All translation logic runs locally in-game, no external APIs
- âœ… **Automated Processing** - Automatically translates messages when confidence is sufficient
- âœ… **Intent Detection** - Understands player intent (LFG, invites, ready status, etc.)
- âœ… **Safe & Conservative** - Stays quiet when confidence is low to avoid wrong translations
- âœ… **Performance Optimized** - Translation caching and message throttling prevent lag
- âœ… **Configurable** - Choose which chat channels to translate
- âœ… **Titan Panel Support** - Optional integration with Titan Panel

### Design Philosophy

This addon is focused on providing **the best translation possible**. It prioritizes clarity and accuracy, stays silent when confidence is low to avoid incorrect translations, and works quietly in the background without disrupting gameplay.
